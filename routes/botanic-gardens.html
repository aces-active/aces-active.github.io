The Singapore Botanic Gardens

The Singapore Botanic Gardens has been inscribed as a UNESCO World Heritage Site on 4 July 2015. The Gardens is the first and only tropical botanic garden on the UNESCO’s World Heritage List. The present site began in November 1859. Over the years, the Gardens has played important roles in germinating the S.E.A rubber industry and served as a cradle for the commercial orchid breeding industry in Singapore. Besides its role in cultivating botanical knowledge, it also played a significant role in the fostering of racial harmony. It was the first site chosen for the Aneka Ragam Ra'ayat (Malay for "People’s Variety Show") in 1959, where Chinese, Malays, and Indians took the stage together for the first time and performed a variety of cultural items.
