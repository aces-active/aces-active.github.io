In the Area

The Quarry

Singapore’s granite core is exposed during the last century by quarrying. Typically, the excavated area in front of a quarry face will fill up with rainwater. in the face of this long abandoned quarry. The outcrop is of lower to mid Triassic around 200 million years old. In Bukit Batok, this feature has been preserved as a tranquil lake, providing home to native wildlife. The park also has a memorial plaque identifying the site where Japanese soldiers built a shrine at the top of Bukit Batok to commemorate their dead. This is close to the television transmission tower which now dominate the summit.
